Here you can search for the demon you need! Other filters such as minimum and maximum tier and the official difficulty are also here for you to find demons to beat!

The tiers filter supports numbers with decimals. If you enter one, the system will check for the 2 d.p. tier of the demon. Otherwise, if you enter an integer, the system will check for the normal tier. The system sorts demons by the order of ID. Check the box if you want it to sort by Recent.
